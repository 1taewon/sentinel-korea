# Walkthrough: Advanced Analytics & Global Expansion

This update introduces advanced health intelligence features and global data visualization to Sentinel Korea.

## New Features

### 1. 🌐 Expandable Global Globe
- **Action**: Click on the mini-globe in the top-right corner.
- **Effect**: Expands to a full-screen interactive globe.
- **Data**: Visualizes real-time signals from **HealthMap**, **ProMED**, and **Google Trends**.
- **Interaction**: Rotate and zoom to explore global disease outbreaks.

### 2. 📈 8-Week Trend Analysis
- **Action**: Click on any Korea region (e.g., 서울특별시) to open the detail panel.
- **Insight**: A dynamic 8-week trend line shows the trajectory of the alert score, helping identify whether a situation is improving or deteriorating.

### 3. 🤖 AI Composite Risk Diagnosis
- **Action**: Scroll down in the region detail panel.
- **Feature**: Uses simulated Gemini analysis to synthesize wastewater, notifiable disease, and search trend data into a concise narrative.
- **KDCA Report**: Generates a one-page report summary ready for submission to the Korea Disease Control and Prevention Agency.
